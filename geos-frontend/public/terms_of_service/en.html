There are three different types of profiles to access the Site: Teacher, School, and Educational Network. The 'Teacher' profile is designed for schoolteachers, who have the opportunity to register and answer the questionnaire that allows them to assess their digital skills and generate an individual feedback report with recommendations and formative resources. The 'School' profile is designed for school principals, who can register and complete the questionnaire for the institution where they are assigned as principal. The collected data provides important information to help the principal plan and implement actions to improve the use and integration of digital technologies for pedagogical purposes in their school.
